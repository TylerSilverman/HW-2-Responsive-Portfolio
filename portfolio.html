Photo 1

This photo shows me preparing to eat Chinese Hotpot. Hotpot is a traditional Chinese meal that is prepared with a base that can be spicy, mild, or have any flavor that interest your taste palette. It is prepared with raw meat and vegetables. All the food products must first go inside the soup base and cook for a few minutes depending on type of food it is. Some of the meats may take longer then others and its the same with vegetables. After the food is cooked you take it out and eat on a separate plate. The soup base is continually cooking items as you take them out and add more items into unti lthe meal is finished.
